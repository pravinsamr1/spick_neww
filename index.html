About Spick

Welcome to Spick Tank Cleaners, your trusted partner in maintaining clean and hygienic water storage systems. We specialize in water tank cleaning, sump cleaning, and underground tank maintenance, ensuring your water stays pure, safe, and free from harmful contaminants. With a team of trained professionals and advanced cleaning equipment, we provide efficient, eco-friendly, and affordable cleaning solutions for both residential and commercial properties. Our step-by-step cleaning process includes draining, sludge removal, high-pressure washing, and disinfection — leaving your tanks spotless and bacteria-free.

At Spick Tank Cleaners, we believe that clean water means a healthy life. That’s why we’re committed to delivering timely, reliable, and quality service that our customers can depend on. Whether it’s a small household tank or a large industrial sump, we handle every job with care and precision.
